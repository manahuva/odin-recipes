Beef Tartare

Picture: Beef Tartare

Ingredients:

  • Raw beef tenderloin
  • Egg yolk
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Bread slices
  • Butter or oil for toasting

Steps:

  1. Finely chop the raw beef tenderloin.
  2. Dice the onion and crush the garlic.
  3. Mix the beef with mustard, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Shape the tartare on a plate and place an egg yolk on top.
  5. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve the beef tartare with toasted bread and enjoy.
