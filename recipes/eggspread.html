Egg Spread

Picture: Egg Spread

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Bread or rolls

Steps:

  1. Boil the eggs and let them cool.
  2. Peel the eggs and chop them into small pieces.
  3. Finely chop the onion.
  4. Mix the eggs with mayonnaise, mustard, and butter.
  5. Add the onion, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Spread the mixture on bread or rolls.
  7. Serve fresh and enjoy.
